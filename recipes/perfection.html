Gelatin? In a Salad? Why Not?

I know I'd said gelatin in a salad but this may be better described as salad in some gelatin

You're probably wondering what's in there, so here's the ingredients

  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatin
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (12 oz) apple juice
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cup finely shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 can (4 oz) chopped pimiento

Alright lets get this salad prepped!

  1. In a small saucepan, combine gelatin, sugar, and salt. Mix Well!
  2. Add 1 cup water. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar and gelatin are dissolved
  3. Remove from heat
  4. Stir in apple juice, lemon juice, vinegar, and 1/4 cup cold water.
  5. Pour into medium bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour or until mixture is consistency of unbeaten egg white
  6. Add carrot, celery, cabbage, green pepper, and pimiento; stir until well combined
  7. Pour into decorative 1 and 1/2 quart mold of your choosing!
  8. Refridgerate 4 hours or until firm
  9. To Unmold, run a small spatula around edge of mold and invert onto serving plate.
  10. Enjoy!
