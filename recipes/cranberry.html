The Candle you never knew you didn't want

You'll be sure to really set the mood at your next get together with thise one

Ingredients!

  • 1 1-lb can Ocean Spray Whole Cranberry Sauce
  • 1 3-oz pkg of red, yellow, or orange fruit-flavored gelatin
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise
  • 1 apple or orange, peeled and diced (you ever dice an orange before? nows your chance)
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 box of birthday candles

Lets make some cranberry candles now huh?

  1. Heat cranberry sauce
  2. Strain and set berries aside
  3. Dissolve gelatin into that hot juice and boiling water
  4. Add salt and lemon juice to the mix
  5. Chill until thick enough to mound slightly when dropped from spoon
  6. Beat in mayo with rotary beater until light and fluffy!
  7. Fold in those cranberries, apples or oranges, and nuts
  8. Divide mixture evenly into eight 6 ounce jars, I know you've got some laying around you hoarder you
  9. Chill 4 hours or longer
  10. Jiggle those jellies (candles) out of their molds
  11. Garnish with another dallop of mayo, why not
  12. Stick those birthday candles in the top
  13. Now light those cranberry candles up and sing a song while your friends look at you in horror while you're serving!
