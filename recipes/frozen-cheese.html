Frozen Cheese Salad!

Wondering why you'd ever make this? Me too!

buckle up kids we'll figure it out together; here's the ingredients.

  • 2 2/3 cups cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons chives
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
  • 1 teaspoon barbecue sauce (REALLY?)(yup)
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire (Wooshtasheer) sauce
  • 2 cups raw broccoli florets
  • Green pepper strips for garnish

Alright now that we've got our ingredients together, lets freeze some cheese!

  1. Let cheese stand at room remp for 30 min
  2. Place in blender container with buttermilk, chives, lemon peel, and sauces
  3. Blend it up until mixture is real smooth
  4. Transfer to freezer tray
  5. Freeze at least 3 hours
  6. Remove from freezer 15 min before serving, gotta let this slab thaw
  7. Unmold from freezer tray onto serving platter
  8. Surround with broccoli florets, gotta make it appear healthy
  9. Garnish with green pepper strips to give em the ol' razzle dazzle
  10. Call your mom and ask if she's proud of you, I know I am
  11. I hope you invited some frineds over cuz this bad mammajamma is big enough to serve 8!
